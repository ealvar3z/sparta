### sparta (barebones tiling wm)

A very lean and spartan tiling wm that does one thing: put pixels on the screen. 

Inspiration came from:

    - jorisvink ce
    - tinywm
    - wmii

Use at your own peril; PRs are welcome!

1. luajit
1. shxkd
